Our School

All Saints' Anglican Academy, All Saints' Cathedral, Kota Kinabalu - inaugurated in Sept 2006.

The Academy is situated on the 4th, 5th, 6th and 7th floors of Wisma Anglican, next to All Saints Cathedral, and currently consists of 190 students, 25 teaching/supervisory staff and 3 part-time teacher. Our present facilities include 7 learning centres able to accommodate up to 30 students each, 3 teaching classrooms, 1 science room, 1 library, 1 assembly hall, 3 general lobbies, 1 privilege room, 2 staff rooms and 3 offices. We also have the use of the field and basketball court in the church compound for our PE and sports activities.

Our students' grade levels range from grade 1 to grade 12. Since 2014 we have a pre-school Learning Centre for pre-schoolers aged 4-5 years and a music room on the 4th floor.
